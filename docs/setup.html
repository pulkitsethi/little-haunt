Prep Scratch before Session 1

Do this alone (or with a parent) before you sit down with her. Setup friction kills the fun.

Checklist

  • Open scratch.mit.edu in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
  • Create a Scratch account with a parent email (she picks the username)
  • Confirm the email if Scratch asks
  • Click Create once so the editor loads
  • Allow sound permissions
  • Bookmark the editor
  • Optional: headphones ready for later sessions

Account tips

  • Username ideas: Haunted[HerName], GhostKitty12, LittleHaunt
  • Parent manages the password; store it safely
  • Keep the project private until she’s ready to share

On the table

  • Laptop or Chromebook (mouse helps with blocks)
  • 30–45 minutes
  • This kit on your phone, or the Session 1 page printed

You’re ready when…

You can open the Scratch editor in under 30 seconds and she can start drawing immediately.
