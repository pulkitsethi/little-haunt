Session 1: Name it, draw it, move

30–45 min. End with a hero she can walk through a dark place — and show someone.

Opening (2 min)

  1. “What’s scary in your game?”
  2. “Who are we?”
  3. “What are we running from?” (monster can wait until Session 2)

A — Dark backdrop (8 min)

  1. Scratch → Create
  2. Click the Stage → Backdrops
  3. Paint near-black or deep green; add a hallway, door, or trees
  4. Rename backdrop: haunted

B — Draw the hero (10 min)

  1. Delete or hauntify the default cat
  2. Paint her hero; rename the sprite to the hero’s name
  3. Keep the size readable

C — Arrow-key movement (12 min)

Select the hero. She snaps; you point:

when green flag clicked
forever
  if <key (up arrow) pressed?> then
    change y by (5)
  if <key (down arrow) pressed?> then
    change y by (-5)
  if <key (left arrow) pressed?> then
    change x by (-5)
  if <key (right arrow) pressed?> then
    change x by (5)

Play after every direction works.

D — Save + name (3 min)

Click “Untitled” → her game title → Save now.

Closing

“Want to show someone that you can walk through the haunted place?” Stop while it’s still fun.
