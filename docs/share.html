Share the spooky game

Sharing is half the fun. Warn about mild scares first.

Before anyone plays

Say: “It’s a little spooky — Halloween scary, not nightmare scary. Ready?”

Turn volume down for younger siblings if needed.

  1. Open the project → Share when she’s proud
  2. Copy the link
  3. Text family: I made a horror game! [link] — green flag, arrow keys.
  4. Or keep it private and play live at the table (often best at age 10)
